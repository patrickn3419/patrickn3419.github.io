Interests


Music

I have always had a passion for music. I enjoy listening to music because it can convey many emotions and messages so well. I have 5 years of self taught piano experience, along with 2 years of lessons. I have around 10 months of guitar experience. I also enjoy playing on the drums occasionally because I learned drums in 6th grade. I like to listen to to 90s rock, 90s hip hop, pop, classical, jazz, and electronic music. On the piano, I enjoy to compose a lot, although I haven't made any full songs yet.

Drawing

I have been drawing for a long time. I enjoy drawing anime the most, however, I also enjoy drawing landscapes and and cartoony styles. I made raised money for my 8th grade feild trip by selling charicatures in middle school. At the end of the year, I was elected as the most artistic person in the grade. Drawing for me is a way to just relax and let my mind rest. I mainly use either pen and pencil, however I have been trying to get into painting and also using oil pastels.

Martial Arts

Martial arts has always been a massive part of my life. I started out doing taekwondo at five years old until I took a two year break from martial arts to do soccer during first and second grade in the suberbs. Returning to Chicago I joined a Karate school called Thousand Waves and I trained there for the next five years, earning my first black belt. I continued to come to class for another few months and taught in the junior class. I then quit to join wrestling and am still training. I believe that martial arts are so amazing because they keep your body in shape and they also teach you very important values in life.

Coding

I picked up coding during the lockdown of 2020. I had a lot of opportunity to experiment with coding. I originally started on Scratch.mit, and I coded short animations. I also coded simple clicker games and platformers which I enjoyed doing because it was satisfying to put the code together and see the result in the end. Then, in my 8th grade year, I was part of the Lego League robotics team in my middle school. Coding is a usefull skill to have as computers are going to start becoming more necessary in modern society. As of now, I'm taking the Web Development course at Lane tech. Coding projects that I have worked on include the collection of websites in which I showcase a recipe.
