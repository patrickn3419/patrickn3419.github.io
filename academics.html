Academic Acheivments

I have always excelled in the academics of my school. The most tricky part about academics is being able to balance in extracurriculars like sports and other learning out side of school along with free-time and sleep. However, I have managed to mantain a steady balance with my academics, even after joining wrestling in the fall. I believe that time management is one of the most important things that a person can learn. It helps a lot to have a structure to my day, and being organized allows me to get the most important work done at the right times so I never miss a deadline.

Some of my favorite subjects are reading, math, mandarin, and web Development. I like reading because when I write essays, I enjoy building an essay from scratch and watching as it unfolds. I like math because I pick up concepts realatively quickly, and I put them to use well. I like mandarin because learning a language is fascinating to me. I like web development, or any other computer science class because it is really easy to pick up and understand, and it can be built upon really easily.
